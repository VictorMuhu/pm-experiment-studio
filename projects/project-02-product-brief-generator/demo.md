# Demo — Product Brief Generator

## Current Status

This project is in the lab phase. There is no demo yet.

The system is being built in phases. A stable end-to-end flow does not exist until Phase 3 is complete.

---

## What the Demo Will Show

When ready, the demo will walk through the following:

1. **Inputs** — three files of raw product data: customer signals, metric snapshots, experiment notes
2. **Normalization** — the inputs are cleaned and structured into a consistent schema
3. **Synthesis** — an LLM reasons across the normalized inputs and identifies the most important themes, movements, and connections
4. **Brief generation** — a structured product brief is produced with customer themes, metric observations, experiment insights, risks, and recommended actions
5. **Evaluation** — the brief is scored against a quality rubric; weak sections are flagged

The demo should feel like watching a PM's weekly synthesis workflow compressed into a repeatable, AI-assisted process.
